# Epic: Voice Inquiries

**Phase**: 4
**Parent**: [Product Roadmap](../ROADMAP.md)

## Objective

Enable users to interact with the chatbot using their voice for a hands-free experience. This involves integrating Speech-to-Text (STT) and Text-to-Speech (TTS) capabilities into the application.

## Key Deliverables

- As a user, I want to click a microphone button to start speaking my question to the chatbot.
- As a developer, I need to integrate a Speech-to-Text (STT) service to convert user audio into text.
- As a developer, I need to integrate a Text-to-Speech (TTS) service to convert the chatbot's text response into audio.
